## The thesis (the reframe that does the work)
It is **not** "purity vs. engagement." It is **which engagement playbook**, and you pick by audience:
- **The casual-app playbook** (mass-beginner habit apps): manufactured-return levers — streak-*guilt*, leagues/ranks, variable/loot rewards, push-nag, minute-goals. Built for users who need a reason to come back.
- **The fitness-tracker playbook** (serious-athlete apps): **forgiving** consistency, **personal-record (PR) detection**, structured multi-week **programs**, post-workout **share cards**, opt-in/ignorable challenges. Built for an intrinsically-motivated, results-driven, anti-cynical audience.

**Virtuoso's audience is the fitness audience** (serious, self-directed, anti-SaaS — documented in the market lane's discovery memory), and Workout mode *literally is a workout*. The fitness playbook engagement-maxxes **aggressively** and is **loved, not resented**, by exactly this user. **So the purity flex barely needs spending:** the most aggressive *proven* retention mechanics for this audience are also the most *authentic* ones. The cynical levers are rejected not on purity but on **ROI** for this audience + plugin (lower return, actively repellent).

**Two structural caps** (true regardless of intent — confirmed by the host-check): a contained plugin has **no push-notification surface** (can't re-engage when closed) and **no social graph** (host scoring is local-only, N≈5–20) → true leaderboards/leagues/push are off the menu by construction, not by choice.

**The efficiency kicker:** the **end-of-Workout recap shipped in Workout-love Tier 3 is the canvas** the whole layer bolts onto. Three of four lanes independently landed there; gamification argues the recap is "half-built" until per-block credit fires.

## The converged build (ranked, with lane backing)
### Tier A — the core loop (all 4 lanes; bolts onto the recap; highest ROI)
1. **Per-block credit — the wiring gap (foundational).** Capture per-block results at each seam (`_activeSession.blocks[]`); run the SAME clean-pass gate **per block** (`advancePathwayTier`/`advanceDepthLadder` against the block's own ref), **strict-evidence-only**: a Workout block flips a tier/rung ONLY on the strict real-mic path (≥`PT_MIN_JUDGED`, ≥65%, proof-held for pilots) — the **lenient mic-less self-confirm a solo run allows is FORBIDDEN in a Workout** (the player flowed through, didn't attest); **zero bonus** (no multiplier/completion-bonus/perfect-streak); **one ledger** (writes `pathway_tiers` + `progress.byNode` exactly like a solo run → `nodeProgressState` shows it with no new surface); **honest partial coverage** (a raw `SEGMENT_TEMPLATE` with no `PATHWAYS` map credits XP + "practiced" only, never a fabricated flip). *Gamification's anti-inflation spine — the inflation risk runs toward too-lenient auto-credit, so the gate tightens, never loosens.* This lights the recap's earned-line, the recognizers, and PRs.
2. **PR detection / "your number," gated on a CLEAN pass.** Guitar-ped's "a number to beat tomorrow" + the market lane's durable D30 engine. Surface the PR on the recap and **seed it into the next session** ("Sweep block — last time 88, beat it"). Extends the existing `fastest`/`best` recognizers. **Track consistency, not just peak; a bad day never nukes it** ("showed up" is separate from "beat your number").
3. **Persisted woodshed log / cross-session history.** Gamification's #1 untapped lane + guitar-ped's #2 + bass-ped's #3 + the market progress-graph. The recap stops evaporating; aggregate REAL artifacts, gained-only: time-on-skill (`sessions`), best clean tempo (`pathway_tiers.bestBpm`), keys traveled (`progress.byNode.keysCleared`), grooves owned (bass). **Upward-only comparison, no decay, no quota, no vanity rank.** The "building over weeks" surface a per-session recap can't be.

### Tier B — habit + commitment (market + gamification)
4. **Forgiving practice STREAK + a UNIT-based daily goal.** The single biggest DAU lever, in its non-toxic form: a day counts when you complete a **real unit** (a Workout block / a clean rung) — **never** app-open, **never** minutes. Built-in **1 rest day/week + a streak freeze**. **Celebrate the return ("welcome back"), never punish the break ("you lost 40 days").**
5. **Named, saveable ROUTINES you own** ("My morning warmup" = a returnable object; extend preset CRUD + `_workoutDraft`) + a **competence-shaped come-back invitation** on Workout entry (points at the growth edge, never loss; changes no state; free to ignore). *Gamification's top studio-feel move (autonomy + identity + ritual).*
6. **Structured multi-week PROGRAMS** — a named arc ("2 weeks to clean alt-picking @120") that pre-commits a sequence of Workouts/rungs; **generator-backed** (a sequence of *specs* re-derived to the player's rig/level, not content-locked); **opt-in**; always hands off (next program / a Jam capstone), **never dead-ends** (avoids the graduation cliff).

### Tier C — social proof + table-stakes
7. **Post-Workout SHARE CARD + cooperative Discord challenge** — the recap becomes copy-able/screenshot-able; a light periodic community challenge. **Cooperative (share-and-cheer), NEVER a ranked leaderboard** (empty/sad at tiny-N); no auto-post (consent). Doubles as the research instrument at tiny-N.
8. **Visible XP LEVEL + competency BADGES** — make the existing XP's *levels* legible as skill-tiers; collectible badges for competency *events*. **Badges name SKILLS not rounds**; keep behind the existing **XP-Off / Casual / Hardcore** switch (the switch itself signals respect). *This is where the "purity flex" actually gets spent — table-stakes presence, the closest to the cynical line.*

## The instrument divergence (guitar ≠ bass — must respect)
- **Guitar:** the denomination of progress is the **clean-tempo PB** ("your number"). Score-shaped, individual, the metronome number guitarists already track.
- **Bass:** a bassist's reward is **felt, not scored** — and the scorer *can't see groove* (~40% of the Core bass ladder is below the 70 Hz mic floor; per-note early/late feedback *induces* the worst pocket habit). So **the bass engagement surface cannot be a score.** The bass denomination is **hold-tempo-in-the-pocket + "grooves you own" + a felt verdict (Locked / Settling / Dragging / Rushing** from drift-variance, never a %). **Plus bass-ped's #1, cheap and high-impact: make the backing DRUMMER the SPINE of the bass Workout** (run groove/walking/application blocks over the backing with the bass stem dropped — the backing engine + `jam-drops-bass` already exist; wire as the bass Workout default). Converts "lesson + timer" into "a session with a rhythm section."
- **Synthesis rule:** credit **time-feel / repertoire-owned / sustained-hold** for bass; **clean-tempo / cold-pass / retained-skill** for guitar. Never speed-as-headline or note-accuracy-as-headline for bass.

## The dial (how far to lean — for the record)
- **On-brand CORE (all 4 endorse, low repel-risk):** per-block credit, PR/your-number, woodshed log, forgiving streak, named routines, programs, share card, bass drummer-spine.
- **Purity-flex SPEND (table-stakes, mild repel-risk, behind XP-Off):** visible XP level number, competency badges.
- **BACKFIRE — do NOT build, even with purity relaxed (all 4 veto):** competitive leaderboards / leagues / feeding the host's flat best-score board; variable/loot-box rewards; punishing streak-guilt (loss-aversion); push-notification nagging; minute-based daily goals; per-note accuracy as a headline; any score on a bass groove; per-note early/late "you're 12ms late" flashes (bass-ped vetoes hardest — actively harmful).

## Proof metrics (read without optimizing a vanity number)
- **Activation:** % of first sessions that COMPLETE one full Workout (a real unit), not just open.
- **Return:** D1/D7/D30 + sessions/week. Read **D7** as the streak/goal signal, **D30** as the PR-graph/program signal.
- **Anti-metric pair:** always read time-in-mode + sessions/week *against* clear-rate-at-spec (catches dwell-padding).
- **Skill-transfer proxies (matter most):** % of Workout completions followed by a Jam same-sitting; a just-drilled skill used unprompted in Custom/Jam; the dogfood 4-line transfer question.
- **Streak-health:** median *real units per credited day* — if it falls toward one minimal session, the streak is manufacturing junk → back off.
- **Honest-reading rule:** NEVER headline streak-length / XP-total / time-on-app. The headline is **"sessions/week with a real unit completed"** + **"clean-clear-at-spec / PR-rate rising."**
